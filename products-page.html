Aglaonema Wishes

Category: Live Plants

Embrace color with this unique Aglaonema cultivar, Wishes. A fitting name since the Aglaonema is said to bring luck, fortune, and general positivity to those who grow it. Whether you believe in its hidden powers or not, there’s no denying this colorful plant will bring joy to your space.

Plant Care

  • Sunlight: Medium bright indirect light
  • Water: Water every 1-2 weeks

Calathea Vittata

Category: Live Plants

This beginner-friendly Calathea is known for its green leaves with brushstroke-like cream stripes. A member of the prayer plant family, Marantaceae, the Calathea Vittata will raise and lower its leaves from day to night as a part of its circadian rhythm. It is a beautiful and unique pet-friendly houseplant.

Plant Care

  • Sunlight: Bright indirect to low light
  • Water: Water every 1-2 weeks
  • Note: Pet friendly

Ficus Teneke

Category: Live Plants

The Ficus elastica ’Tineke’ – or variegated rubber tree – is a popular variety of ficus known for its beautiful pink, green, and yellow leaves. The Tineke’s striking variegation and easy-going nature make it the perfect new plant to add to your sill this year.

Plant Care

  • Sunlight: Bright direct to indirect light
  • Water: Water every 1-2 weeks

Monstera Deliciosa

Category: Live Plants

Nicknamed the “swiss cheese plant”, the Monstera deliciosa is famous for its quirky natural leaf holes. These holes are theorized to maximize sun fleck capture on the forest floor. Depending on the season and maturity of the plant, your Monstera could arrive with no holes just yet, and be sized to grow alongside you.

Plant Care

  • Sunlight: Bright to indirect to medium light
  • Water: Water every 1-2 weeks

Pink Anthurium

Category: Flowers & Botanicals

This bright pink Anthurium lives up to its nickname, the flamingo flower. Rarely without their showy blooms, Anthuriums are know as the world’s longest blooming houseplant Each bloom can last up to eight weeks, and new ones will pop up often. These aren’t actual flowers, but modified waxy leaves. Anthuriums flourish and bloom best in bright indirect light.

Plant Care

  • Sunlight: Bright to indirect to medium light
  • Water: Water every 1-2 weeks

Watercolor Purple Orchid

Category: Flowers & Botanicals

Watercolor orchids are Phalaenopsis orchids dyed using a safe and natural method. You may notice a small amount of blooms on your orchid upon delivery. These blooms will open quicker in a warm indoor setting. Phalaenopsis orchids typically bloom about once a year, for up to three months. After a blooming cycle, the flowers will wilt and fall off.

Plant Care

  • Sunlight: Bright indirect
  • Water: Water every 1-2 weeks

White Orchid

With its elegant stems and vibrant white flowers, this Phalaenopsis orchid makes any space feel more chic. You may notice a small amount of blooms on your orchid upon delivery. These blooms will open quicker in a warm indoor setting. The Phalaenopsis will typically bloom about once a year, for up to three months.

Plant Care

Petite White Orchid

Category: Flowers & Botanicals

Affectionately nicknamed the beginner orchid, the popular Phalaenopsis orchid is one of the easiest varieties of orchids to grow as a houseplant. This orchid will typically bloom once a year for up to three months. After a blooming cycle, the flowers will wilt and fall off and the orchid will store up energy to re-bloom again next season.

Plant Care

  • Sunlight: Bright indirect
  • Water: Water every 1-2 weeks
